ADHM WITH DIPLOMA

ADHM (Advanced Diploma in Hotel Management)

Duration: 21 months (15 months study + 6 months internship)

This smart course has been designed for “+2 graduates”. Any student who has completed “+2” from any faculty scoring a GPA of 1.6 is eligible to learn this course.

a) Advance Diploma in Hotel Managemet.
b) French language
c) Driving classes.
d) Personality development
e) Working exposure in local market
f) Field Visit to different 5-star hotels in Nepal
g) More than 100 practicals in all 4 different departments.
h) Free study materials for Hotel Management

Completion of course qualifies you for a internship of 6 months and than start your career working in reputed five star properties, job placement is managed by the college in both Nepal and abroad. Our students are working worldwide. After completing your internship of 6 months you can take also credit transfer and complete bachelors in just 2 or 3 years which is also know as lateral entry to bachelors in Nepal or most developed countries like America, Australia etc. The whole process shall be managed by the college.
